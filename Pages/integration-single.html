Connect Integration

Overview

Connect Dark X with Slack to receive real-time notifications, collaborate with your team, and streamline your workflow. Get instant alerts for important events directly in your Slack channels.

Key Features

  • Real-time Notifications

    Receive instant alerts for important events, alerts, and updates in your Slack channels.

  • Channel Customization

    Choose which notifications go to which channels for better organization.

  • Direct Messages

    Get personal notifications sent directly to you or your team members.

  • Interactive Actions

    Take action on notifications directly from Slack without switching apps.

How It Works

1

Connect Your Workspace

Authorize Dark X to access your Slack workspace with OAuth.

2

Configure Notifications

Choose which events trigger notifications and where they go.

3

Start Collaborating

Receive updates and take action from directly within Slack.
