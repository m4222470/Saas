John Carter

CEO & Founder

John is a serial entrepreneur with over 20 years of experience in the technology industry. He founded Dark X in 2018 with a vision to make enterprise-grade technology accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Before Dark X, John held leadership positions at several Fortune 500 companies and successfully exited two startups. He holds an MBA from Stanford and a BS in Computer Science from MIT.
